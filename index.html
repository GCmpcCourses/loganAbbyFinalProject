Spotify Wrapped

Spotify Wrapped is a viral marketing campaign by Spotify. Released in early December every year since 2016, the campaign allows Spotify users to view a compilation of data about their activity on the platform over the past year, then invites them to share it on social media.

The 2021 Controversy

Many Spotify users this year were expecting something different than what they got. Spotify seemed to have some odd genres such as aggrotech, bubble trance, new weird america, jerk, and wonky. When these genres came up on people's wrapped, they were very confused. Along with some weird genres, people were making fun of the graphic design.
